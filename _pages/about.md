I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the [University of Udine](https://www.uniud.it), Italy, working in the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. My research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Responsible AI, with a focus on Large Language Models (LLMs), political bias detection, misinformation assessment, and trustworthy evaluation of AI systems.

My work explores both foundational aspects of LLM behaviour—such as measuring and influencing political ideology in language models—and applied problems in healthcare, including conversational agents for patient information and AI-assisted analysis of medical reports.

Before starting my PhD, I earned my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Udine.

**Research interests:** Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, Responsible AI, Information Access, AI in Healthcare, Misinformation Detection, Benchmark Evaluation
