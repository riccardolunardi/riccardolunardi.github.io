I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the [University of Udine](https://www.uniud.it), Italy, working in the MITEL and SMDC Laboratories. My research lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Clinical AI, with a primary focus on building trustworthy AI systems for healthcare.

To this end, I investigate foundational aspects of LLM behaviour, including reliable evaluation methodologies, LLM personas and agent-based systems, political bias detection, and misinformation assessment, and apply these insights to clinical problems such as AI-assisted analysis of medical reports and conversational agents.

Before starting my PhD, I earned my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Udine, during which I also worked as a Data Engineer at [Retail Shake](https://www.retailshake.com) gaining hands-on experience in large-scale data pipelines and real-world data infrastructure.

**Research interests:** Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, Responsible AI, Information Access, AI in Healthcare, Clinical Coding, Misinformation Detection, Benchmark Evaluation
